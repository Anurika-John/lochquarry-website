Welcome To Locquarry
Adventure

Welcome to Lochquarry Outdoor Centre, Scotland’s home of unforgettable outdoor adventure for young people aged 6–18. Set in the stunning Argyll hills, we offer a huge range of land‑based, water‑based and rope‑based activities designed to build confidence, teamwork and a love for the outdoors.
